Intro

Software architect, software engineer, software developer and coder: the latter is the least skilled but Coder's Dungeon sounds better than Engineer's Dungeon.

Coders here may learn something about programming and computer science, and even some engineers and architects that I know too...

I do not pretend to be the best software developer/engineer/architect/whatever in the World, I just aim to be better than I am now, to learn new things and exchange knowledge with other people.

You can't know what you can learn from people...

Have fun. KernelPanicX
